FAQ.

Does EZ TMS integrate with QuickBooks?

Yes. EZ TMS offers a two-way integration with QuickBooks, allowing you to seamlessly sync both Accounts Receivable (AR) and Accounts Payable (AP). This helps reduce manual data entry, improve accounting accuracy, and keep your financial data up to date.

Do I need technical knowledge to use EZ TMS?

No. EZ TMS was built by freight brokers for freight brokers. Our platform is designed to be intuitive, easy to learn, and simple to implement. Automated workflows and user-friendly tools help teams get up and running quickly without requiring extensive technical expertise.

Does EZ TMS offer an LTL solution?

Yes. EZ TMS includes a comprehensive LTL portal that provides access to both direct carrier rates and marketplace pricing. This allows your team and customers to efficiently quote, book, and manage LTL shipments from a single platform.

How is EZ TMS different from other TMS software providers?

At EZ TMS, we believe software is only part of the solution. Our partnership-first approach means we are invested in the long-term success of your brokerage. We prioritize relationships, customer support, and business growth over simply selling subscriptions.

What does support look like after implementation?

Our support doesn’t end after onboarding. Every customer has access to our dedicated support team for ongoing assistance via phone and email. Whether you need training, troubleshooting, or guidance on best practices, we’re here to help every step of the way.
